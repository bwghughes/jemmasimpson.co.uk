About the Author

Meet Jemma Simpson

Jemma is an experienced NHS registered general nurse, health visitor and school nurse, qualifying as a specialist community public health nurse.

Through her years of caring for others, Jemma has gained unique insights into wellbeing and what truly contributes to lasting happiness. In The Middle, she shares the wisdom that comes from helping people through life's challenges—showing readers that happiness isn't found at the extremes, but in the balanced space within.

The Book

The Middle: Happiness is Found Within

In a world that constantly pulls us toward extremes—success or failure, joy or despair, perfection or inadequacy—we often overlook the most powerful place of all: the middle.

This book is an invitation to discover the profound contentment that awaits when we stop seeking happiness outside ourselves and begin the journey inward.

The Middle is a self-help guide focused on supporting the emotional wellbeing of teenagers. It introduces a mix of strategies, such as mindfulness and cognitive behavioural therapy, to promote healthy habits and positive relationships.

  • Practical wisdom for finding balance in everyday life
  • Reflections on embracing life's uncertainties with grace
  • Gentle guidance for cultivating inner peace
  • A transformative perspective on what happiness truly means
